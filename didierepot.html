About Me

I am a student at Agahozo-Shalom Youth Village in Rwanda, pursuing an Advanced Level education in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Beyond academics, I'm deeply involved in community activities, serving as a committee member in the Critical Thinking for Peace club and as a pianist in the ASYV Band.

My leadership experience includes coordinating club activities and serving as the Inspire Me vice president. I'm also passionate about sports, having played on both the volleyball and football teams. I'm proficient in multiple languages including Kinyarwanda, English, Swahili, and French, and have been recognized with awards in Model United Nations events.
